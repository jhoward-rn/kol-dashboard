About this tool
KOL Engagement Tracker is a centralized planning tool for monitoring, segmenting, and managing interactions with Key Opinion Leaders. It gives your team a shared view of each KOL's advocacy status, engagement history, and influence across geographies.
  • Segment KOLs by advocacy and belief level — Vocal Advocate, Quiet Champion, Cautious Skeptic, and Unconvinced Leader
  • Plan and track engagements by quarter with completed vs. planned visibility
  • Score influence across clinical, geographic, and online dimensions
  • Visualize segment evolution over time with the interactive timeline slider
  • View regional coverage by country with tier and segment breakdowns
  • Compare KOLs side-by-side across key attributes
  • Filter and sort by segment, tier, country, specialty, and engagement status
